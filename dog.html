Essential Needs

  • 🐶 Feed high-quality dog food twice daily
  • 💧 Always provide fresh water
  • 🏃 Daily exercise (walks/playtime)
  • 🛁 Regular grooming & nail trims
  • 💉 Yearly vet checkups & vaccinations

Training Tips

  • Start with basic commands (sit, stay, come)
  • Use positive reinforcement
  • Be consistent with rules
  • Socialize with people and other dogs
